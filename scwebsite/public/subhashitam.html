Welcome to Subhashitam Samskritam


  • Subhashitam Samskritam is an event organized as a collaboration between Sanskrit Club of IIT Roorkee and Samskrita Bharati.
  • It is an effort to help the society to be able to converse in Sanskrit by adopting a unique methodology of teaching through Subhashitas.
